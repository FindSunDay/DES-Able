DES

Disability Employment Services

DES is the Australian Government’s employment service that helps people with disability, injury or health condition in receiving assistance to prepare for, find and keep a job.

What are DES services ?

DMS

Disability Management Service

  • Job finding assistance for
    people with disability, injury or health condition. 
  • Occasional, short-term supports in workplace.

ESS

Employment Support Service

  • Job finding assistance for
    people with permanent disability.
  • Ongoing, long-term supports in workplace.
